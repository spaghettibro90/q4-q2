CHARACTERS IN NOLI ME TANGERE
Juan Crisostomo y Ibarra
  • He is the young, wealthy son of Don Rafael Ibarra who has returned to San Diego after 7 years of studying abroad in Europe.
  • He is an earnest, idealistic, cultured, and well-respected.
Maria Clara de los Santos
  • She is the daughter of Kapitan Tiago and Dona Pia Alba, but she is actually the illegitimate daughter of Padre Damaso, who engaged in sexual relations with the latter. She was raised by Tia Isabel in absence of Pia Alba, who died giving birth to her.
  • She is sweet, beautiful, kindhearted, and well-loved.
Padre Damaso
  • He is a Franciscan friar and the former curate of San Diego before being replaced by Padre Salvi. As it turns out, he is the real father of Maria Clara, having engaged in sexual relations with Dona Pia Alba.
  • He is seen as arrogant, ruthless, pedantic, and acts superior to others with his power as friar.
Kapitan Tiago
  • He is a businessman and socialite in San Diego, the husband of Pia Alba, and the father of Maria Clara. He owns multiple land there and became an influential businessman.
  • He is wealthy, submissive, well-respected, and has multiple social connections.
Padre Salvi
  • He is the town curate of San Diego, succeeding his predecessor Padre Damasox.
He is seen as meticulous, punctilious, manipulative, and has rivalries with the alferez of the Guardia Civil, and a wanting for Ibarra's death.
